# Gary's AI Survival Guide

I wrote this guide because there is so much hopelessness, confusion, and hype around AI. I believe God called me to help people navigate this moment with clarity, practical wisdom, and faith.

This is not a technical manual. It is a philosophy for thriving in the AI economy, grounded in the belief that you are not made to be irrelevant. Whether you are a business owner, a professional at a crossroads, or someone just trying to figure out what is next, this guide is for you.

Read it start to finish, or jump to whatever chapter speaks to where you are right now.
